James Carr

James Carr is a second-year EECS major. He grew up in southern California in Orange County, which with a population of over 170,000 Muslims constitutes the largest Muslim community in California. His interests outside Electrical Engineering and Computer Science include trombone, electronic music, and Chipotle.
